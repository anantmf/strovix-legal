# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** May 27, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how IPTV MAC STALKER PLAYER ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") collects, uses, discloses, and otherwise processes personal information in connection with our mobile application ("App") and related services.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Please read this Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Portal Credentials:** When you connect to an IPTV portal, we collect the server URL and authentication credentials you provide. These credentials are used solely to authenticate with your selected IPTV provider and are stored locally on your device.
- **User Profiles:** We store profile names and settings on your device to support multi-profile functionality.
- **Preferences & Settings:** App preferences, theme selections, TV focus settings, and other customizable options are stored locally.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Playback History:** The App tracks which content you play, including:
  - Title and ID of watched content
  - Current playback position and duration
  - Timestamp of last view
  - Content type (VOD, live channel, or series episode)
  - This information is stored locally on your device to enable the "Continue Watching" feature.

- **Live Channel History:** We track recently watched live channels to populate the "Recently Watched Live Channels" section. This data is stored locally.

- **Broken Stream Tracking:** The App records which streams fail to load or encounter errors to help improve service quality and detect problematic content sources.

- **Device Information:**
  - Operating System and version (iOS/Android)
  - Device model and manufacturer
  - Device memory and storage information
  - Screen resolution and size
  - Unique device identifiers (for ad targeting purposes only; see Advertising section)

- **App Usage Data:**
  - Features accessed
  - Buttons clicked and screens viewed
  - Session duration and frequency

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties

- **IPTV Provider:** Any information retrieved from your configured IPTV portal (channel lists, content metadata, account status, expiration dates) comes directly from the portal you connect to. We do not store this information beyond caching it temporarily for performance purposes.

## 3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

### 3.1 Google Mobile Ads SDK

The App uses **Google Mobile Ads SDK** to serve advertisements. Google may collect and process personal information to:

- Display targeted ads based on your interests and device characteristics
- Measure ad performance and user engagement
- Prevent ad fraud

**Types of Ads:**
- Banner ads on the Home screen and content catalogs
- Interstitial ads between live channel switches
- Rewarded video ads for ad-free viewing periods

**Google's Privacy Policy:** https://policies.google.com/privacy

**Opt-Out:** You can opt out of personalized ads through Google's ad preferences:

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage

The majority of your data (playback history, profiles, settings, credentials) is stored **locally on your device only**. This data does not leave your device unless you explicitly sync or back up your data through your device's native backup system (Google Drive for Android, iCloud for iOS).

### 4.2 Transmission & Encryption

- Connections to IPTV portals use encrypted protocols (HTTPS/TLS) to protect your credentials and data in transit.
- Ad requests and analytics data may be sent over encrypted connections to third parties.

### 4.3 Data Security

While we implement security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable precautions to protect your information.

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Playback History

- Playback history and recently watched channels are retained until you manually clear them or delete the App.
- Clearing app data from your device settings will remove all stored history and preferences.

### 5.2 Ad-Free Status

- When you watch a rewarded ad to unlock ad-free viewing, the status is stored locally for up to 4 hours from the time of watching.

### 5.3 Other Information

- IPTV portal credentials and settings are retained until you delete them or uninstall the App.
